## Integrated Risk Assessment

- **Bootstrap Safety**: Unconstrained self-modification could lead to runaway changes
- **LLM Dependency**: Single provider dependency creates vulnerability
- **Complexity Management**: Self-referential nature may cause unpredictable behaviors
- **Coverage Gaps**: Critical modules like checkpoint.py have 0% test coverage

### Market Risks
- **Adoption Barrier**: Paradigm shift requires significant developer mindset change
- **Competitive Response**: Major players may replicate key concepts
- **Regulatory Scrutiny**: Self-modifying systems may face compliance challenges
- **Timing Risk**: Market may not be ready for such radical innovation

### Mitigation Strategies
- **Implement Bootstrap Governance**: Approval workflows and rollback capabilities
- **Enhance Multi-Provider Support**: Abstract LLM dependencies
- **Strengthen Learning Loops**: Automatic template extraction from successful executions
- **Add Chaos Engineering**: Proactive resilience testing
